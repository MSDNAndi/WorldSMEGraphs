## Key Features of Enhanced Design

### Narrative Structure
- **8-Beat Story Spine**: Panels 1-32 map to Hero's Journey
  - Beat 1 (P1-2): Ordinary World (overconfident start)
  - Beat 2 (P3-5): Call to Adventure (confronting complexity)
  - Beat 3 (P6-8): Refusal (overwhelmed, seeking help)
  - Beat 4 (P9): Meeting Mentor (Socratic guidance)
  - Beat 5 (P10-16): Crossing Threshold (innovation breakthrough)
  - Beat 6 (P17-24): Tests (reality checks)
  - Beat 7 (P25-28): Inmost Cave (ethics, consolidation)
  - Beat 8 (P29-32): Return (transformation, inspiration)

## Parent's Note ğŸ‘¨â€ğŸ‘©â€ğŸ‘§â€ğŸ‘¦

**What This Teaches:**

This story introduces the fundamental concept of **time value of money** (NPV) at a toddler-appropriate level through:

- **Immediate gratification vs. delayed rewards** - Basic intertemporal choice
- **Quantity comparison** - Foundation for value assessment  
- **Opportunity cost** - Simple trade-offs (toy now vs. two toys later)
- **Present bias** - Natural preference for immediate rewards
- **Counting and comparison** - Pre-math skills

**Key Learning Outcome:** Understanding that "when" you get something matters, not just "what" you get - the foundational insight behind discounted cash flow analysis.

## The Shortest Distance: Planck Length ğŸ“

Imagine you have a ruler.

Could you cut that tiny space in half?

Yes! And you could cut it in half again.

**Keep cutting for a really long time...**

After you cut it in half 120 times, you'd get close to something called an "atom."

Atoms are the tiny building blocks that make up everything around you!

But Planck length is WAY smaller than even an atom!

**How much smaller?**

If you took an atom and cut it into a million pieces...

You'd FINALLY be close to Planck length!

**Why can't we go smaller?**

Scientists think that Planck length might be the smallest distance that makes sense.

Trying to measure something smaller is like trying to count something smaller than 1!

# Version 11: "Networking: The Multiplayer Experience"

## Story Concept
Marco realizes the MVP Summit is like an MMO—you can solo it, but it's way more fun (and sustainable) in a party. He and Andreas form a "wellness guild" that optimizes for both networking AND health.

## Theme
Social connection as wellness, team-based accountability, multiplayer mindset

## Character Notes

### Marco Casalaina (Friendlier Characterization)
- **Natural party leader**: Charismatic, inclusive, makes everyone feel welcome
- **Gaming enthusiasm**: Genuinely loves the MMO metaphor
- **Supportive teammate**: Celebrates others' wins as his own
- **Witty banter**: "LFG water break, healers welcome"
- **Active listener**: Remembers party members' wellness goals

### Andreas Erben
- **The healer class**: Provides support buffs (hydration reminders, snack runs)
- **Guild wisdom**: Shares experience from many "raids" (summits)
- **Quiet strength**: Tanks the hard conversations about pacing

1. **Server Selection**: Arriving at the summit, choosing your "build"

3. **Dungeon Runs**: Navigating sessions, expo hall, networking events
4. **Raid Boss**: The evening party—managing social energy and alcohol

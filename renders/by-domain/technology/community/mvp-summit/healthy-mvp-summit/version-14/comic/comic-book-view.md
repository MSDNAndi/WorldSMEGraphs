### The Five Assassins Defeated:

| Assassin | Threat | Defense |
|----------|--------|---------|
| **The Jitters** | Caffeine overconsumption | 90-min delay, moderate dose, 6-hour cutoff |
| **The Night Thief** | Alcohol-disrupted sleep | Max 2 drinks, stop 4 hours before bed |
| **The Brain Drain** | Decision fatigue | Pre-decide trivial matters |
| **The Energy Vampire** | Social energy depletion | Schedule recovery time |
| **The Phantom** | FOMO | Three priorities, full presence |

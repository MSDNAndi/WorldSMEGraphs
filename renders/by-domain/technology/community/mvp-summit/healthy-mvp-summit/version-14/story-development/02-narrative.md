### Panel 20
*Educational "case file" insert panel. Styled like a detective's evidence board. Shows a brain diagram with adenosine receptors blocked by caffeine molecules, and a building pile of adenosine behind the block.*

**CASE FILE: THE JITTERS - BIOLOGICAL PROFILE**

**MECHANISM**: "Caffeine blocks adenosine receptors. Adenosine = brain's 'tired' signal. BUT adenosine continues building up behind the block."

### Panel 36
*Educational "case file" insert. Shows decision fatigue as resource depletion.*

**CASE FILE: THE BRAIN DRAIN - COGNITIVE ROBBERY**

**THE SCIENCE**: "Decision-making draws from a LIMITED cognitive resource. The brain doesn't distinguish trivial from important - each decision costs the same."

**THE TIMELINE**:
- "Morning: 100% capacity"
- "Each decision: -5-10%"
- "By afternoon: <20%"

**THE SYMPTOMS**: "Decision avoidance, impulsive choices, defaulting to easiest option, analysis paralysis"

**THE DEFENSE**: "Pre-decide trivial matters. Save capacity for what matters."

# Healthy MVP Summit V3 - Story Idea

## Title: "The Summit Survival Handbook: Lessons Learned the Hard Way"

## Version: 3.0
## Style: 1950s American Comic Book (with educational infographic elements)

---

## Educational Objectives

### Focus Areas (Unique to V3)
1. **Travel Recovery** - Pre-flight, during flight, post-flight
2. **Food Danger Zones** - Buffet psychology, late-night snacking
3. **Sleep Science** - Circadian rhythms, melatonin, light exposure
4. **Social Battery** - Introvert/extrovert energy management
5. **Decision Fatigue** - Reducing choices to preserve energy
6. **Conference FOMO** - Managing fear of missing out
7. **Post-Summit Recovery** - Going home and bouncing back
8. **Long-term Health** - Building habits that last
9. **Emergency Fixes** - When everything goes wrong
10. **Buddy System** - Finding your healthy summit partner

---

## Character Specifications

### Andreas Erben (Protagonist 1)
- Same visual description
- **Narrative role**: The reformed summit veteran who learned from mistakes
- **Arc**: Shares past failures to teach present wisdom

### Marco Casalaina (Protagonist 2)
- Same visual description
- **Narrative role**: The curious learner who asks good questions
- **Arc**: From skeptic to convert

### Ensemble Cast
- **Past Andreas** (younger version in flashbacks)
- **Various suffering MVPs** (cautionary tale examples)
- **Helpful Microsoft staff** (showing campus resources)

---

## Story Hook

Through a series of "I wish I'd known" flashbacks and real-time demonstrations, Andreas teaches Marco (and the reader) the complete summit survival guide - from booking flights to post-event recovery.

---

## Central Theme

"Every health mistake at a conference is a lesson. The wise person learns from others' mistakes instead of repeating them all."

---

## Story Arc (HIGH LEVEL ONLY - NO PANELS)

### Act 1: The Journey
- The flight strategy conversation
- Airport and travel tips
- Jet lag pre-game moves
- Arrival and initial choices
- The first critical 24 hours

### Act 2: The Conference Gauntlet
- Food strategy with buffet psychology
- Hydration and caffeine science
- Social energy management
- The dreaded afternoon slump
- Emergency recovery techniques

### Act 3: The Evening Challenge
- Dinner decision making
- Alcohol and social pressure
- The late night temptation
- Sleep hygiene in hotels
- Next-day preparation

### Act 4: The Recovery
- Final summit day energy
- Post-summit flight strategy
- Going home recovery plan
- Building lasting habits
- Finding your summit buddy

---

## Style Notes

### Visual Emphasis
- Flashback panels (sepia tones, wobbly borders)
- Split panels: WRONG WAY vs. RIGHT WAY
- Infographic-style educational inserts
- "Danger zone" warning panels
- Humor through exaggeration of bad choices

### Tone
- Educational but funny
- Self-deprecating humor from Andreas
- Practical, immediately applicable advice
- Emphasis on "we've all been there"

---

## Estimated Scope
- Target: 45-48 panels
- Mix of present-day and flashback panels
- Educational infographic inserts
- "Do this, not that" comparison panels

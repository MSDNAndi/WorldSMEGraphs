## Visual Style Summary

### 1950s Comic Elements
- Bold black outlines throughout
- Ben-Day dot shading for depth
- Vintage color palette (limited, saturated)
- Classic rounded speech bubbles with tails
- Rectangular caption boxes for narration
- Sound effects in dynamic vintage lettering

### Sci-Fi Elements
- Crackling energy portals (like vintage pulp sci-fi)
- Glowing auras around variants (distinct colors)
- Timeline tinting (each variant's scenes in their color)
- Dissolution effects (shimmering fade)
- Time vortex tunnels

### Emotional Progression
- Panels 1-5: Normal → Shock
- Panels 6-14: Fear → Learning
- Panels 15-24: Concern → Understanding
- Panels 25-33: Overwhelm → Simplification
- Panels 34-40: Exhaustion → Balance
- Panels 41-47: Anxiety → Peace
- Panels 48-53: Satisfaction → Hope

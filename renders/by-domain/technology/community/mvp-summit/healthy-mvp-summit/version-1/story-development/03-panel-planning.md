# Healthy MVP Summit V1 - Panel Planning

## Overall Strategy

### Target panel count: 48 panels
### Pacing philosophy: 
- Balance between dialogue-heavy panels and action panels
- Every 3-4 dialogue panels, include a visual/action panel
- Key educational points get dedicated visual panels

### Key moments requiring their own panel:
- Character introductions
- Each major health tip demonstration
- Campus beauty shots for visual variety
- Emotional beats (friendship moments)
- Educational diagrams/illustrations

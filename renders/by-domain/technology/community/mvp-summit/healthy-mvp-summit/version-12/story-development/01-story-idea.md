# Version 12: "The Wellness Quest Log"

## Story Concept
The MVP Summit transforms into an epic fantasy adventure where Marco (the enthusiastic bard) and Andreas (the wise wizard) must complete wellness side quests to achieve the ultimate goal: surviving the summit with energy to spare.

## Theme
Adventure, discovery, playful approach to serious health topics

## Character Notes

### Marco Casalaina (Friendlier Characterization)
- **The Bard class**: Charismatic, entertaining, boosts party morale
- **Enthusiastic quester**: Genuinely excited about each challenge
- **Witty commentary**: Narrates their adventures with humor
- **Loyal companion**: Sticks with party through tough times
- **Quick wit**: "I put all my points into Charisma and none into Sleep"

### Andreas Erben
- **The Wizard class**: Wisdom, knowledge, strategic planning
- **Quest giver**: Introduces new wellness challenges
- **Patient mentor**: Guides without condescending
- **Hidden depths**: Reveals knowledge at crucial moments

## AKU Links
- All 10 conference wellness AKUs as "side quests"
- Each quest unlocks a health benefit (XP = wellness points)

## Quest Log
1. **Hydration Quest**: Collect 8 glasses of water throughout the day
2. **Movement Quest**: Take 10k steps across the conference center
3. **Sleep Quest**: Achieve 7+ hours of rest despite jet lag
4. **Social Quest**: Make 5 meaningful connections without burnout
5. **Focus Quest**: Maintain attention through 3 consecutive sessions

## Narrative Arc
1. **Quest Accepted**: Marco sees the summit as an RPG adventure
2. **Side Quests**: Various wellness challenges as fun mini-games
3. **Boss Battles**: Overcoming temptations (late nights, skipping meals)
4. **Leveling Up**: Each healthy choice unlocks new abilities
5. **Victory**: Completing the summit with bonus XP (energy to spare)

## Character Notes

### Marco Casalaina (Friendlier Characterization)
- **Personality**: Enthusiastic, charismatic, genuinely curious about people
- **Snarky but warm**: His quips come from affection, not criticism
- **Listens actively**: Despite talking a lot, he remembers what people tell him
- **Natural connector**: Introduces people, remembers names, builds bridges
- **Bay Area optimism**: "We can solve this together" attitude

### Andreas Erben
- **Personality**: Warm mentor, observant, patient teacher
- **German heritage**: Occasionally drops German phrases (Gesundheit!, Prost to water!)
- **Florida resident**: Comments on Seattle weather vs Florida sunshine
- **Quiet leader**: Leads by example, not proclamation

## üìñ Page 2: The Heeler Family Morning

![Page 2: The Heeler Family Morning](./bluey-images/image_002_20260104_210133_4d7b7c14.png)

Bluey and Bingo were playing "Hospital" in the backyard when Mum called out, "Girls! We're going to visit Grandad at the hospital today!"

"Is Grandad okay?" asked Bluey, looking worried.

"He's going to be just fine," said Dad, giving her a reassuring hug. "A special doctor is helping his blood vessels work better."

---

## üìñ Page 3: What Are Blood Vessels?

![Page 3: What Are Blood Vessels?](./bluey-images/image_003_20260104_210134_f2cab167.png)

"Dad, what's a blood vessel?" asked Bingo, tilting her head.

Dad knelt down to explain. "You know how we have roads and streets for cars? Well, your body has roads too! They're called blood vessels, and blood travels through them like tiny cars."

"Woooow!" said Bluey. "Roads inside us!"

---

## üìñ Page 4: The Heart is the Engine

![Page 4: The Heart is the Engine](./bluey-images/image_004_20260104_210127_87d7f302.png)

"Your heart is like a really powerful engine," Dad continued. "It pumps blood through all these roads, delivering oxygen and food to every part of your body."

Bingo put her paw on her chest. "I can feel it! Thump-thump-thump!"

"That's your engine running," Dad smiled. "It never takes a break!"

---

## üìñ Page 5: Red Roads and Blue Roads

![Page 5: Red Roads and Blue Roads](./bluey-images/image_005_20260104_210133_187116f3.png)

"There are two types of roads," Mum explained in the car. "Red roads called arteries carry fresh blood FROM your heart. Blue roads called veins bring tired blood BACK to your heart."

Bluey nodded thoughtfully. "So it's like... delivery trucks going out and garbage trucks coming back?"

"Exactly right, Bluey!" said Mum.

---

## üìñ Page 6: Arriving at the Hospital

![Page 6: Arriving at the Hospital](./bluey-images/image_006_20260104_210228_c301bb14.png)

When they arrived at the hospital, Bluey was amazed by how big it was. There were people in colorful scrubs walking around, and the air smelled like clean soap.

"This is where the special doctors work," said Dad. "They help people when their blood vessel roads need fixing."

---

## üìñ Page 7: Meeting Dr. Vena

![Page 7: Meeting Dr. Vena](./bluey-images/image_007_20260104_210238_91854732.png)

In Grandad's room, they met Dr. Vena, a kind vascular surgeon with warm eyes.

"Hello Heeler family! I'm Dr. Vena. I'm taking care of Grandad's blood vessels."

"Are you fixing his roads?" asked Bingo.

Dr. Vena laughed kindly. "That's a wonderful way to put it! Yes, one of Grandad's leg roads had a blockage, and we cleared it out."

---

## üìñ Page 8: Grandad Explains His Procedure

![Page 8: Grandad Explains His Procedure](./bluey-images/image_008_20260104_210236_f5690fa5.png)

Grandad held out his paws for hugs. "Come here, you two! I'm feeling so much better now."

"What happened to your road, Grandad?" Bluey asked.

"Well, over time, some stuff built up inside the road, making it hard for blood to get through to my leg. Dr. Vena used a special tiny tool to clear it all out!"

---

## üìñ Page 9: The Body's Amazing Highway System

![Page 9: The Body's Amazing Highway System](./bluey-images/image_009_20260104_210237_ff5c9d7b.png)

Dr. Vena showed them a colorful poster of the body's blood vessel system.

"Look at all these roads! Your body has about 60,000 miles of blood vessels‚Äîthat's enough to wrap around the Earth more than twice!"

"That's HEAPS!" exclaimed Bluey.

"And your heart pumps blood through all of them, every single day," Dr. Vena added.

## üìñ Page 10: Playing the "Blood Cell" Game

![Page 10: Playing the Blood Cell Game](./bluey-images/image_010_20260104_210239_82a692d7.png)

Back home, Bluey had an idea. "Let's play 'Blood Cells'!"

She set up a course in the living room. "I'm an oxygen truck! I carry oxygen FROM the heart to the toes!" She ran around the couch.

"I'm bringing carbon dioxide back!" yelled Bingo, running the other direction.

Mum and Dad watched, smiling at their daughters' imaginative play.

---

## üìñ Page 11: When the Roads Need Help

![Page 11: When the Roads Need Help](./bluey-images/image_011_20260104_210331_a19fda40.png)

During the game, Bluey put cushions in the pathway. "Oh no! There's a blockage!"

"The traffic is backing up!" Bingo pretended to be stuck.

Dad joined in. "Time to call... Dr. Vena!" He pretended to move the cushions. "There! The road is clear again!"

"YAY!" the girls cheered as they resumed their running.

---

## üìñ Page 12: Grandad Comes Home

![Page 12: Grandad Comes Home](./bluey-images/image_012_20260104_210338_53e2e190.png)

A few days later, Grandad came home from the hospital. He walked into the backyard where the girls were playing.

"Grandad! Your roads are working again!" Bluey hugged him tight.

"They sure are! And because of that, I can play with you two properly now."

Grandad kicked a ball across the grass. "Catch it, Bingo!"

---

## üìñ Page 13: Keeping Our Roads Healthy

![Page 13: Keeping Our Roads Healthy](./bluey-images/image_013_20260104_210341_08d6f2c1.png)

That night at dinner, Mum served vegetables and fish.

"Eating healthy food helps keep your blood vessel roads clean and strong," she explained.

"Like... road maintenance!" said Bluey.

"Exactly," Mum nodded. "And exercise is like making sure the roads get used regularly, so they stay in good shape."

---

## üìñ Page 14: Exercise is Fun!

![Page 14: Exercise is Fun!](./bluey-images/image_014_20260104_210339_9350075b.png)

The next day, the whole family went to the park. They ran, jumped, played fetch, and had races.

"My heart is pumping SO FAST!" panted Bluey happily.

"That means your blood is zooming through all your roads," Dad said. "Your body loves this!"

Even Grandad joined in for a gentle walk. "Got to keep these new roads working!"

## üìñ Page 17: Thank You, Dr. Vena

A week later, Grandad had a follow-up appointment. The whole family went along.

"Your roads are looking excellent!" Dr. Vena told Grandad. "Keep up the healthy eating and walking."

Bluey tugged on Dr. Vena's coat. "Thank you for fixing Grandad's roads. You're like a superhero!"

Dr. Vena knelt down. "And YOU'RE a super granddaughter. Take care of those roads of yours!"

**Learning Objectives:**
1. Understand basic cardiovascular anatomy (heart, arteries, veins)
2. Recognize that blood flows in a circuit through the body
3. Learn that doctors can help fix blood vessel problems
4. Understand lifestyle choices that promote vascular health
5. Reduce fear about hospitals and medical procedures

**Discussion Questions:**
1. "What does Bluey's heart do?"
2. "What's the difference between red roads and blue roads?"
3. "How did Dr. Vena help Grandad?"
4. "What can we do to keep our blood vessels healthy?"
5. "Why was Bluey worried at first, and why was she happy at the end?"

## ğŸ“– Print Instructions

### Materials Needed:
- A3 or 11x17 inch paper (or tape two A4/Letter sheets together)
- Color printer
- Scissors (optional for trimming)

### How to Fold:
1. Print this document on large paper
2. Cut out the 6 panels
3. Arrange in order: 1-2-3-4-5-6
4. Tape or glue panels together in a row
5. Accordion-fold so Panel 1 (Cover) is on front

### Alternative: Standard Booklet
1. Print panels on 6 separate half-sheets
2. Staple along the left edge
3. Creates a traditional page-turning booklet

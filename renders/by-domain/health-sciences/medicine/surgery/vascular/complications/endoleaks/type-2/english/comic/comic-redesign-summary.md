## Problem Statement

Current 32-panel comic about Type 2 endoleak research suffers from:
1. **Linear fact delivery** with no emotional journey
2. **Flat characters** without development arcs
3. **No challenges or obstacles** to drive engagement
4. **Missing interactive elements** (reader is passive)
5. **Unclear emotional beats** (flat enthusiasm throughout)

**Result**: Educational but not engaging; informative but not inspiring

---

## Solution Overview

Transform into emotionally engaging learning journey using:
- **8-beat story spine** (Hero's Journey for research)
- **Character growth arcs** (3 distinct personalities develop)
- **Challenge-based learning** (obstacles drive deeper understanding)
- **Interactive elements** (13 reader engagement prompts)
- **Emotional scaffolding** (4 peaks, 4 valleys, clear transformation)

**Result**: Educational AND inspiring; scientifically rigorous AND emotionally resonant

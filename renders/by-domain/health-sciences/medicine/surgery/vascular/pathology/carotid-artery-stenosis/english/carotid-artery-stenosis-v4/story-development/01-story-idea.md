# Carotid Stenosis V4 - Story Idea

## Title: "Camilo's Research Project"

## Main Characters

### Dr. Young Erben
- **Role**: Vascular surgery researcher and educator, mentor figure
- **Age**: 35
- **Appearance**: Professional lab coat, confident warm smile, dark hair

### Camilo
- **Role**: Research intern (Colombian heritage)
- **Age**: 23
- **Appearance**: Curly dark hair, analytical expression, often taking notes
- **Personality**: Methodical, research-oriented, loves understanding mechanisms

### Supporting Characters
- Diego and Camila (fellow interns, brief appearances)
- Mrs. Yamamoto (patient, 68-year-old retired professor with TIA history)

## Story Premise

Camilo is assigned to research carotid artery disease for a departmental presentation. Through studying cases, observing duplex ultrasounds, and witnessing a patient's TIA workup, he gains deep understanding of stroke prevention and the importance of screening.

## Educational Goals

1. **Primary**: Understand carotid stenosis and stroke risk
2. **Secondary**: Learn duplex ultrasound interpretation
3. **Tertiary**: Differentiate symptomatic vs asymptomatic disease management

## Emotional Arc

- **Opening**: Camilo excited to dive into research
- **Rising Action**: Studying cases, learning ultrasound interpretation
- **Turning Point**: Meeting Mrs. Yamamoto, recent TIA survivor
- **Resolution**: Understanding the real-world impact of timely intervention
- **Closing**: Camilo delivers successful presentation, feels like a scholar

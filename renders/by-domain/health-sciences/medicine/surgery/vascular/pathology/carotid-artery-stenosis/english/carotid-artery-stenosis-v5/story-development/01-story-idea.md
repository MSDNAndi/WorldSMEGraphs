## Story Premise

Camilo is working on a research paper about stroke prevention when a patient arrives after experiencing a TIA. Dr. Young Erben guides him through understanding carotid artery disease, interpreting duplex ultrasound findings, and learning about intervention criteria. The case provides real-world data for his research.

## Educational Goals

1. Understand carotid artery disease and its relationship to stroke
2. Learn to interpret carotid duplex ultrasound findings
3. Recognize TIA symptoms and their significance
4. Understand intervention criteria and treatment options

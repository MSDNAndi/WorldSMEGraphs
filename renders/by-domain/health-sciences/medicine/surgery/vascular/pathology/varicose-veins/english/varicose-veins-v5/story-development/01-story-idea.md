## Story Premise

Camila spends a day in the vein clinic with Dr. Young Erben, learning about varicose veins from patient consultations. She discovers the occupational risk factors, learns to differentiate between cosmetic concerns and medical necessity, and develops her patient communication skills.

## Educational Goals

1. Understand the pathophysiology of varicose veins
2. Recognize risk factors including occupational hazards
3. Learn conservative vs surgical treatment options
4. Develop patient counseling skills

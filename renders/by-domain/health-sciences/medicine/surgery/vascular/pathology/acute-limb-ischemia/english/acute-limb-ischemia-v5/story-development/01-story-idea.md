## Story Premise

Diego is shadowing Dr. Young Erben in the emergency department when a patient arrives with sudden severe leg pain. This is Diego's first exposure to acute limb ischemia, and Dr. Erben guides him through recognizing the "6 Ps" of arterial occlusion while managing the time-critical intervention.

## Educational Goals

1. Recognize the classic presentation of acute limb ischemia
2. Understand the "6 Ps": Pain, Pallor, Pulselessness, Paralysis, Paresthesia, Poikilothermia
3. Appreciate the time-critical nature of intervention
4. Learn about diagnostic workup and treatment options

## Unique Narrative Elements

- Diego's perspective as a nervous but eager learner
- Dr. Erben's calm teaching style under pressure
- The urgency of the "golden hours" for limb salvage
- Diego's growth from observer to active participant

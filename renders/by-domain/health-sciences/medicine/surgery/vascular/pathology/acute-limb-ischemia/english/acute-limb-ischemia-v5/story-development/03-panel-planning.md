# Acute Limb Ischemia V5 - Panel Planning

- Setting: Hospital corridor
- Characters: Dr. Young Erben (petite Korean female, 35, lab coat) and Diego walking together
- Action: Dr. Erben speaking encouragingly to Diego

- Setting: ER nursing station
- Characters: Nurse calling out, Dr. Erben and Diego turning

### Act 2: The Six Ps (Panels 7-14)

**Panel 7: Teaching Moment - Temperature**
- Setting: Bedside
- Characters: Dr. Erben guiding Diego's hand to patient's leg
- Action: Diego touching the pale leg
- Dialogue: "Feel the temperature difference"
- Educational: POIKILOTHERMIA
- Mood: Learning

**Panel 8: Diego's Observation**
- Setting: Close-up of hands on leg
- Characters: Diego's hands comparing legs
- Action: Diego touching both legs
- Dialogue: "The right leg is much colder"
- Educational: Temperature comparison
- Mood: Discovery

**Panel 9: Pallor Assessment**
- Setting: Bedside, full view of both legs
- Characters: Dr. Erben pointing to color difference
- Action: Visual comparison of leg colors
- Dialogue: "Notice the color—that's Pallor"
- Educational: Second P - PALLOR
- Mood: Teaching

**Panel 10: Checking Pulses**
- Setting: Close-up of ankle
- Characters: Dr. Erben demonstrating pulse check
- Action: Fingers on dorsalis pedis artery
- Dialogue: "Let's check pulses"
- Educational: PULSELESSNESS
- Mood: Focused examination

**Panel 11: Diego Tries**
- Setting: Close-up
- Characters: Diego feeling for pulse
- Action: Concentrated expression, feeling ankle
- Dialogue: "I can't find it"
- Mood: Learning through doing

**Panel 12: Confirmation**
- Setting: Bedside
- Characters: Dr. Erben nodding
- Action: Confirming the finding
- Dialogue: "That's because there isn't one—Pulselessness"
- Educational: Third P confirmed
- Mood: Serious realization

**Panel 13: Motor/Sensory Testing**
- Setting: Bedside
- Characters: Dr. Erben testing toe movement
- Action: Patient trying to wiggle toes
- Dialogue: "Can you wiggle your toes?"
- Educational: PARALYSIS & PARESTHESIA
- Mood: Concern

**Panel 14: All Six Ps Identified**
- Setting: Split panel showing all six Ps
- Characters: Dr. Erben explaining to Diego
- Action: Educational summary moment
- Dialogue: "We have all six Ps"
- Educational: Pain, Pallor, Pulselessness, Paralysis, Paresthesia, Poikilothermia
- Mood: Critical understanding

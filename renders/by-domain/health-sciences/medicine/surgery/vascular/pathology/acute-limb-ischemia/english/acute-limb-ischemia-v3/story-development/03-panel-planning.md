## Single-Line Prompt Format

Each panel will have a single-line prompt following this structure:
`[SCENE DESCRIPTION] [CHARACTER DETAILS] [VISUAL STYLE] [EDUCATIONAL FOCUS]`

Style standard for all panels:
- Dora the Explorer cartoon aesthetic
- Thick black outlines
- Flat bright colors
- Simple backgrounds
- Expressive faces
- Medical accuracy in clinical elements

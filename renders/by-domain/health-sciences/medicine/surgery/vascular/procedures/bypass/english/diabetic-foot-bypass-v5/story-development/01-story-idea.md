## Story Premise

All three research interns work together on a complex case: a diabetic patient with a non-healing foot ulcer requiring bypass surgery. This story showcases multidisciplinary care, teamwork, and the comprehensive management of diabetic foot disease.

## Educational Goals

1. Understand the pathophysiology of diabetic foot disease
2. Learn about multidisciplinary care for diabetic patients
3. Understand bypass surgery for limb salvage
4. Appreciate the importance of long-term diabetes management

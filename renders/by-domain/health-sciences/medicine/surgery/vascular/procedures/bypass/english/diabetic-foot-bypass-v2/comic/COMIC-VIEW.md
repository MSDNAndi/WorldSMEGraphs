# Diabetic Foot Bypass - Educational Comic Story

**Character**: Dr. Young Erben (Korean, petite, 40s, multilingual vascular surgeon)  
**Total Panels**: 35

### Panel 1: The Non-Healing Wound

**Scene**: Maria Torres, 62, examining her foot ulcer at home

**Dialogue**: "Llevo tres meses y no sana..." (I've had this for three months and it won't heal...)

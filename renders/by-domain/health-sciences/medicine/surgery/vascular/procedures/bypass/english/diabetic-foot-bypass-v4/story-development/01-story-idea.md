# Diabetic Foot Bypass V4 - Story Idea

## Title: "The Team Challenge"

## Main Characters

### Dr. Young Erben
- **Role**: Vascular surgery researcher and educator, team leader
- **Age**: 35
- **Appearance**: Professional lab coat, confident warm smile, dark hair

### Diego, Camila, and Camilo
- **Roles**: Research interns working collaboratively on a complex case
- **The Team**: Diego (curious, glasses), Camila (enthusiastic, Spanish heritage), Camilo (analytical, Colombian heritage)

### Supporting Characters
- Mr. Washington (patient, 62-year-old diabetic with non-healing foot wound)

## Story Premise

A complex diabetic foot case requires all three interns to collaborate, each contributing their unique strengths. Diego focuses on vascular assessment, Camila handles patient education and wound care coordination, and Camilo researches optimal bypass techniques. Together with Dr. Erben, they save the patient's limb.

## Educational Goals

1. **Primary**: Comprehensive diabetic foot management (vascular, wound care, glycemic control)
2. **Secondary**: Bypass surgery indications and techniques
3. **Tertiary**: Multidisciplinary teamwork in complex cases

## Emotional Arc

- **Opening**: Complex case arrives, Dr. Erben assigns team project
- **Rising Action**: Each intern tackles their area, encountering challenges
- **Turning Point**: Team meeting to synthesize findings
- **Resolution**: Successful bypass surgery and wound healing
- **Closing**: Team celebration, understanding the power of collaboration

## Viewing Formats Explained

### 6-Panel Grid View
Best for: **Quick browsing** and **page-by-page reading**
- Displays 6 panels per page in a 2x3 grid
- Easy to see multiple panels at once
- Good for comparison and overview

### Continuous Story View
Best for: **Sequential reading** and **full narrative experience**
- Shows all panels from start to finish
- Includes dialogue and captions (when available)
- Like reading a traditional comic

### Pictures Only View
Best for: **Visual focus** and **quick review**
- Pure images without text
- Useful for visual learners
- Quick reference for image sequences

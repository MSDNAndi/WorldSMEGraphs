# Pharmacy Domain

This domain contains knowledge related to pharmaceutical sciences, including pharmacology, pharmaceutical chemistry, and drug development.

## Subdomains

- **Pharmacology** - Study of drug action and mechanisms
- **Pharmaceutical Chemistry** - Chemical aspects of drug development
- **Pharmacokinetics** - Drug absorption, distribution, metabolism, elimination
- **Pharmacodynamics** - Drug effects on the body

## Ontological Notes

This domain is the **native domain** for drug-related concepts including mechanisms of action, pharmacokinetics, and pharmacodynamics. Clinical use of drugs in specific conditions belongs in the relevant medical specialty domain, with cross-references to these foundational pharmacology AKUs.

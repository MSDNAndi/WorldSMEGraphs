# Public Health Domain

This domain contains knowledge related to population health, disease prevention, and health promotion.

## Subdomains

- **Occupational Health** - Health and safety in the workplace
- **Epidemiology** - Study of disease distribution and determinants
- **Health Policy** - Policies affecting public health outcomes
- **Disease Prevention** - Strategies for preventing illness at population level

## Ontological Notes

This domain is the **native domain** for public health concepts that apply at the population level. Clinical applications affecting individual patients belong in `health-sciences/medicine/`. Research findings about populations belong here.

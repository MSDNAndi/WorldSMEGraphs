---

## ⚠️ IMPORTANT: Copyright Compliance

**All AKUs must be written with original content.** While we can identify topics and knowledge domains from published sources (textbooks, guidelines, etc.), the actual AKU content MUST be:

1. **Written from scratch** - No copying or paraphrasing from copyrighted materials
2. **Based on general medical knowledge** - Information that exists across multiple sources and represents standard of care
3. **Original in presentation** - Our own language, structure, and pedagogical approach
4. **Properly attributed where needed** - Clinical guidelines may be referenced but not reproduced

**What we CAN do:**
- Identify topic areas that should be covered
- Note that a concept exists and is important
- Create original educational content about that concept
- Reference publicly available classification systems (CEAP, Stanford, etc.)
- Include standardized terminology codes (SNOMED-CT, ICD-10)

**What we CANNOT do:**
- Copy text from Rutherford's Vascular Surgery
- Reproduce SVS/ESVS guideline language verbatim
- Copy algorithms or decision trees without permission
- Use copyrighted diagrams or illustrations

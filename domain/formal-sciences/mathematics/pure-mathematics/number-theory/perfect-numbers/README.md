# Perfect Numbers

## Overview
This domain covers perfect numbers - rare and fascinating integers that equal the sum of their proper positive divisors. Perfect numbers have captivated mathematicians for over 2000 years and remain an active area of research.

## Domain Path
`science/math/number-theory/perfect-numbers`

## Key Concepts
- Definition of perfect numbers
- Aliquot sums and divisor functions
- Euclid-Euler theorem
- Connection to Mersenne primes
- Even vs odd perfect numbers (open problem)

## Known Perfect Numbers
- 6, 28, 496, 8128, 33550336, ...
- Only 51 known as of 2024
- All known perfect numbers are even
- Whether odd perfect numbers exist is unknown

## Relationships
- **Fundamental connection**: Mersenne Primes (Euclid-Euler theorem)
- **Related to**: Abundant numbers, Deficient numbers, Amicable numbers
- **Historical**: Ancient Greek mathematics

## Open Problems
- Do odd perfect numbers exist?
- Are there infinitely many perfect numbers?

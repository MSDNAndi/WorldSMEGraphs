## Key Themes for Book Chapter

### The Dilemmas Framework

The book chapter should be organized around key dilemmas:

1. **The Diagnostic Dilemma**: When to suspect and how to confirm
2. **The Approach Dilemma**: Open vs endovascular
3. **The Viability Dilemma**: What to resect
4. **The Timing Dilemma**: When to intervene
5. **The Resource Dilemma**: Transfer vs treat locally
6. **The Goals Dilemma**: When to pursue palliation

### Key Clinical Pearls

1. "Pain out of proportion" is classic but often absent
2. Normal lactate does NOT exclude early AMI
3. CTA is the diagnostic cornerstoneâ€”order liberally
4. Atrial fibrillation + abdominal pain = AMI until proven otherwise
5. When in doubt about viability, plan second-look
6. Speed of diagnosis matters more than choice of treatment
7. NOMI is the silent killer of the ICU

### Evidence Gaps to Acknowledge

- No RCTs comparing open vs endovascular for AMI
- Biomarkers not yet clinically validated
- Optimal anticoagulation duration for MVT uncertain
- Viability assessment adjuncts improve accuracy but not standardized

## Overview

This knowledge graph represents a comprehensive, state-of-the-art overview of Betriebswirtschaftslehre (BWL), the German term for Business Administration. BWL encompasses all aspects of managing and operating a business enterprise, from accounting and finance to marketing, human resources, operations, and strategic management.

## Lessons Learned

### What Worked Well
1. **Readability validation** - Automated testing caught issues early
2. **Progressive analogies** - Same concepts, increasing depth
3. **Reference documents** - Quick card highly valuable
4. **Clear navigation** - INDEX.md reduces confusion
5. **Metadata files** - Transparency builds trust

### Challenges Overcome
1. **Extreme scales** - Hard to find relatable analogies
2. **Math avoidance** - Elementary/limited without losing accuracy
3. **Consistency** - Ensuring no contradictions across levels
4. **Cultural adaptation** - German needed metric-native examples
5. **Complexity control** - Not introducing concepts too early

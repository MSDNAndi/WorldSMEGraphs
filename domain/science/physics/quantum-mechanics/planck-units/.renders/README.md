## Usage Guidelines

**For Educators**:
- Use these renderings as introductory material before technical content
- Pair with visual aids and demonstrations
- Allow 10-15 minutes for reading and discussion
- Follow up with questions to assess comprehension

**For Self-Learners**:
- Start with your native language version
- Read at your own pace
- Don't worry about understanding everything perfectly
- Focus on the big ideas first
- Re-read sections as needed

**For Translators**:
- Maintain simple sentence structure
- Use everyday vocabulary in target language
- Adapt cultural examples appropriately
- Keep the encouraging, accessible tone
- Validate readability with native speakers

## Usage Guidelines

### Citing NIST Values
Always include:
1. Full citation (Tiesinga et al. 2021)
2. DOI: 10.1063/5.0064853
3. Direct NIST URL
4. Year: 2018 (data) / 2021 (publication)

### Citing Textbooks
Include:
1. Authors, title, year
2. ISBN
3. Specific chapter/section if applicable
4. Publisher

### Citing Papers
Include:
1. Authors, title, year
2. Journal, volume, pages
3. DOI (preferred over URL)

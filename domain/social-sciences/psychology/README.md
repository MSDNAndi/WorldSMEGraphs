# Psychology Domain

This domain contains knowledge related to the scientific study of mind and behavior, organized by subdomain.

## Subdomains

- **Cognitive Psychology** - Study of mental processes including attention, memory, problem-solving
- **Personality Psychology** - Study of individual differences in characteristic patterns of thinking, feeling, and behaving
- **Social Psychology** - Study of how individuals' thoughts, feelings, and behaviors are influenced by others

This domain is the **native domain** for psychological concepts. Concepts are organized by psychological subdomain. Applications in other domains (e.g., workplace productivity, marketing) should link to these AKUs rather than duplicate content.
